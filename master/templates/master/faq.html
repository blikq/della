Shipping Information

How will my parcel be delivered?

We use reputable courier services to deliver your parcels directly to your doorstep. The delivery method and timeline will depend on your location and shipping preferences selected at checkout.

Do I pay for delivery?

Yes, delivery fees apply based on your location. Free shipping may be available on specific promotions or for orders above a certain amount. You can check shipping costs during the checkout process.

Orders and Returns

Tracking my order

You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has been dispatched. Use this number to monitor your parcel’s delivery status on the courier’s website.

I haven’t received my order

If your order hasn’t arrived within the estimated delivery time, please contact our customer support with your order number. We’ll investigate the issue and provide an update as soon as possible.

How can I return an item?

To return an item, contact us within 14 days of receiving your order. The product should be unused and in its original packaging. We’ll provide return instructions and process your refund or exchange.

Payments

What payment types can I use?

We accept payments through Paystack, which supports various payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile money. Additionally, direct bank transfers are also accepted.

I can't make a payment

If you are having issues making a payment, please check your card details, internet connection, and available funds. If the problem persists, contact our support team for assistance.

Has my payment gone through?

Once your payment is successful, you will receive an email confirmation. If you do not receive confirmation or if the transaction is pending, please contact us for support.
